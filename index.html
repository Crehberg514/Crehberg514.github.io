Christopher Rehberg

I am a graduate aerospace engineering student working on the future of space structures and space material designs. I have a strong research background, developed within the Structures and Materials Design Lab at the University of Central Florida. There, I have gained an expertise in time-dependent material, Prony series, and deployable space structures. While an undergraduate researcher in the design lab, I designed libraries in Python and MATLAB to accompany my thesis. Both of these libraries can be found on my GitHub account, or under the projects section.
